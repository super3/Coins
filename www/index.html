About MilliB:

The MilliB service is currently available to Bitcoin related websites that require microtransations. These include faucets, microtasks, pay-to-click, and other websites. We are committed to providing a solution to these websites, so they are not affected by the microtransaction change. Users are free to check their balance. We will provide tools to facilitate microtransactions in the future for everyone.

MilliB API:

We want to make MilliB integration into your website as painless as possible. All you need is a simple one line GET request. 

http://api.millib.com/v1/[secret api key]/send/[bitcoin address]/[amount]

This will send the specified amount to the Bitcoin address out of your MilliB wallet. You can read the full documentation here.
